Our Services

Flower Delivery

Offer same-day, next-day, and scheduled delivery options. Ensure delivery is available for different locations with real-time tracking.

Custom Bouquets

Allow customers to design their own bouquets by choosing specific flowers, colors, and arrangements to suit their preferences or occasions.

Subscription Service

Provide a subscription option where customers can receive fresh flowers on a regular basis (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly) with special discounts.





Event Floral Arrangements

Specialize in creating floral arrangements for events such as weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events, and other special occasions.

Plant and Gift Accessories

Sell complementary products such as potted plants, vases, greeting cards, chocolates, and other gift items to enhance the flower-giving experience.

Floral Workshops and Tutorials

Offer online and in-person workshops teaching customers how to arrange flowers, care for plants, and create floral crafts, fostering a community around your brand.





Corporate Services

Provide corporate clients with regular office flower arrangements, holiday decorations, and special event services with tailored packages and contracts.

Seasonal and Holiday Specials

Create exclusive collections and promotions for holidays like Valentine’s Day, Mother’s Day, Christmas, and other seasonal celebrations to drive sales during peak times.

Eco-Friendly Options

Offer sustainable and eco-friendly products, including organic flowers, recyclable packaging, and options for customers to contribute to environmental causes with their purchases.
